# Commercial licensing

Sbobinator is **proprietary software** by [Antonio Trento](https://antoniotrento.net).

## Who needs a paid license?

A **commercial license** is required if you use Sbobinator:

- inside a **company**, public administration, or organization;
- as a **self-employed professional** in the course of your work;
- to provide **paid or internal services** to clients, staff, or users;
- in any way that goes beyond [free personal / non-profit use](licenses.md).

**Personal, educational, and documented non-profit use remains free.**

## What you are licensing

You pay for a license to **use the Sbobinator application** (UI, worker, packaging, updates).

Open-source **dependencies** (PyTorch, FastAPI, NeMo toolkit, etc.) and **model weights** (NVIDIA Parakeet, Qwen) remain under their own licenses. Your commercial license covers **our product**, not a relicensing of third-party components.

## How to buy

1. Contact **https://antoniotrento.net** with:
   - organization name and country;
   - number of seats or machines;
   - use case (internal, client-facing, SaaS, etc.).
2. You receive a **quote** and a **commercial license agreement**.
3. After payment, you receive confirmation suitable for your compliance records.
